About Apollo Performing Arts

At Apollo Performing Arts & Academy, we're dedicated to teaching students to perform confidently — whether for personal enjoyment, family, friends, or public audiences. Our program typically spans 3–5 years depending on student's age and experience. We offer Regular Lessons and Advanced Lessons, with Performing Teams available by audition.

Our Mission
